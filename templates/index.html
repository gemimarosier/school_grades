Half of these schools are concentrated in 4 counties.

From what we found, approximately 50 percent of these schools are in Dade, Duval, Hillsborough, and Broward counties. What do the worst performing schools have in common? We wanted to find out. In the state of Florida, 179 schools out of 3338 schools received a F grade. The schools are spread across 33 counties. School grades are calculated based on a point system that differs by elementary, middle, and high school. We took a look at some characteristics to help us discover key similarities.
